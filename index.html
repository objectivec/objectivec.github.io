Just the Differences

Parallel Bible translation text without the same words repeated over and over again

The following English Public Domain Bible translations are used:

  1. King James Bible
  2. Young's Literal Translation
  3. American Standard-ASV1901
  4. Bible in Basic English
  5. World English Bible

Privacy Policy:

No user data is collected, sent or received. The JTD app simply displays Bible verses from an internal database.
